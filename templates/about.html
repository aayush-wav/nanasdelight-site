The Artisan's Journey

Deep in the heart of Patan Dhoka, where tradition meets everyday life, Nana's Delight was born from a simple passion for pure, soulful chocolate.

Soulful Ingredients.

Every bar of Nana's Delight tells a unique story with its ingredients and its place of origin. Working with these flavors from across Nepal, we are able to create a truly special chocolate experience.

From the tangy **Himalayan Goji Berries** to the crisp **Mustang Apples**, each ingredient is carefully selected to ensure the warmth and soul of Nepal is present in every bite.

Made to Share.

Our chocolates are crafted for moments of connection. Whether it's a bundle to share with a vegan friend or a gift for a loved one, Nana's Delight aims to spread warmth and joy.
